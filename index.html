📜 Terms & Privacy
1. Service
AbuLink provides instant mobile data purchases for Nigerian networks. Orders are processed by our team as quickly as possible.

2. Payments
All payments are made via bank transfer. Once confirmed, your data will be delivered as soon as possible. No card details are ever stored or required.

3. Refunds
If data is not delivered within 30 minutes, contact us on WhatsApp for a full refund or re-delivery. We do not keep your money for failed orders.

4. Privacy
Your name, phone number and order history are stored locally on your device only. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Account Security
You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. Use the "Forgot Password" option if you lose access.

6. Contact
For any issues, reach us instantly via WhatsApp through the Support tab inside the app.
